VOLUNTEER

Stay involved and help run CSK for the next cohort!

Our six committees work hard throughout the year to keep CSK running. We are continuously working to improve the program by designing new curriculum, hosting industry panels, obtaining more funding, and making CSK accessible to as many people as possible.

Check in later to join a committee!
Events

Events works with other organizations including the EECS Department and other student groups to run CAP events, community events, etc. They also plan and host workshops with industry partners and member socials.

Curriculum

Curriculum continuously improves on the existing curriculum by designing new topics and teaching materials for each incoming cohort of CS Kickstarters. During the week of CSK, they staff labs and workshops.

Publicity & Design

Publicity and Design runs CSK's social media accounts and helps increase its visibility in the department, college, and media. We also design and order CSK swag like stickers and our signature purple t-shirts!

Industrial Relations

Industrial Relations communicates with potential and previous sponsors and applies to grants to help keep CSK completely free of charge. They also assemble and send a resume book to hiring companies.

Website

Website creates and mantains this website by continually improving its features and design and updating new profiles and sponsors every year. They keep updated on CSK's progress and reflect it here.

Finance

Finance serves as a point of contact for companies interested in sponsoring CSK. They make each year's budget and track and document spending. Finance also works with the EECS Department and ASUC including running Big Give.
