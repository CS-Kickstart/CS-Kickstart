SPONSOR US

One of our biggest goals is to increase the accessibility of a computer science education. As a result, our program is free to charge to participants, and we depend on sponsors to keep CS Kickstart possible. Without our sponsors, there would be no way we would be able to host so many students and provide an experience that will positively impact their pursuit of computer science, completely free of charge to eliminate any possible barriers.

PACKAGES

A donation is a wonderful way to support our program! Your gift will help us recruit teachers and guest speakers, provide the students with meals & boarding, develop our curricular materials, and continue to provide students with a fun and comprehensive introduction to the computer science field.
