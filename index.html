OUR PROGRAM

1. Introduces girls to the basics of Python programming, Web Development, and Web Design.

2. Provides an opportunity to visit tech companies in Sillicon Valley to explore and talk with the industry experts.

3. Prepares incoming freshmen to a college life through informal mentorship and a variety of available resources.

4. Creates an empowering community of friends through fun social activities (ice cream social, board games, etc.).

Exposure

You will get the opportunity to visit tech companies in the Bay Area to see what life is like for computer scientists in industry.

Diversity

Participants are all girls coming from different backgrounds to explore the creativity and diversity of computer science.

Community

You will meet around 50 other girls with similar interests, allowing you to form an inclusive community before school starts.
